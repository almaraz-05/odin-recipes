Pizza

Description

This is a famous Chicago style pizza recipe which was created by the famous Josue Almaraz. Having opened the now popular restaurant Sway's Pizza, he has decided to share the recipe with us to make it at home! This recipe makes a 16" deep dish pizza which is loved by thousands.

  • 1 1/3 cups warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons fine salt
  • 1/2 cup cornmeal
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  1. Make the dough
  2. Simmer pizza sauce
  3. Preheat oven
  4. Bake pizza
