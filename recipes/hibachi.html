Hibachi

Description

This is a famous recipe which was created by the famous Josue Almaraz. Having opened the now popular restaurant Sway's Hibachi, he has decided to share the recipe with us to make it at home! This recipe is loved by thousands.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Veggies
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • Chicken
  • Rice

Instructions

  1. Make the rice
  2. Cook veggies
  3. Cook chicken
  4. Add soy sauce and teriyaki sauce
